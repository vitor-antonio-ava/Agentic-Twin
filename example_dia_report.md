Quality Update – RFT Metric Deviation and Proposed Alignment
Hi Team,
Please find below a summary of the current situation and proposed actions related to the RFT metric deviation.

1. Situation
Due to variations in how RFT is calculated across suppliers, a single deviation can lead to the DS RFT metric presenting as red.
For example, in January 2028 there was one final batch from Aplora with a single deviation, resulting in 0% RFT.

2. Current Response
The deviation is being managed through the standard DMAIC process with appropriate corrective actions identified.
This is not a repeat occurrence.
The RFT calculation methodology will be discussed at the next Quality Council meeting with a proposal to count all process steps at Aplora to align with other DS CMOs.


3. Patient Impact
There is no impact to patients.

4. Balanced Scorecard Impact
Updating the calculation methodology will allow for a more accurate representation of the RFT metric within EM DS.

5. D.I.A (Decision / Information / Awareness)

Decision: EM DS Quality Council to confirm the proposed RFT calculation approach.
Awareness: EMLT informed regarding the expected impact on the RFT metric.


6. Recommendation
Align Aplora RFT calculation methodology with other DS CMOs to ensure consistent reporting.

7. Next Steps

Action Owner Timeline
